# Round 005 art pipeline result

The board-led pipeline is implemented and exercised. The generated style board
is the sole visible coherence reference. One individually generated 2 by 2
cottage, one neutral player master, and one complete six-frame east walk grid
were generated against it. The walk grid was accepted or rejected as one unit,
with no frame cherry-picking.

The five-layer early taste gate is `iso-five-asset-spike.png`. Godot composes
the board ground reference, footprint-only cast mask, tight contact mask,
individual cottage, and neutral player. `player-walk-iso-spike.gif` is the
generated east-facing walk preview. `isometric-before-after.png` compares the
previous cardinal capture with the new isometric spike.

`generate2dmap` helped by forcing an in-world dressed board before isolated
asset work. That made palette, object scale, camera, and light direction
concrete. `generate2dsprite` helped by routing the walk to a compact multi-row
per-facing grid tied to one master, avoiding a long strip and a low-resolution
48-cell generation.

The remaining Scott-gated generation is seven complete per-facing walk grids,
the compact flora category sheet, and the remaining individual town buildings.
The manifest, ingest, normalization, shadow, atlas, rejection, GIF, comparison,
and Godot spike producers are ready for those inputs. No missing art is replaced
with mirrored or aesthetically selected frames.
