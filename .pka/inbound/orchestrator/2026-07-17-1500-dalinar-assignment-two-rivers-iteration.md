## The objective: a true "Two Rivers / Emond's Field" starter town

"Two Rivers" is the Wheel of Time (Robert Jordan) setting — for team members
who don't know it, the concrete brief:
- A cozy, isolated RURAL village. Humble but charming, lived-in, warm.
- Thatched and slate-roofed cottages of stone-and-timber (half-timbered) —
  exactly the spike's building style.
- A central larger building (the Winespring Inn analog) anchoring the town,
  like the spike's tavern with the hanging sign.
- Dirt lanes, hedges, kitchen gardens, a village green/commons.
- Surrounded by countryside feel: trees, flowering bushes, rocks, tall
  grass, the flora from the spike. English-countryside-meets-fantasy.
- NO grand castles, no fantasy spectacle. Small, rooted, real.

Definition of done for the arc: the RUNNING, PLAYABLE game renders this town
at spike fidelity — seamless textured ground (not flat color diamonds),
real flora and props, spike-quality buildings and character, all assembled
