traveler, brown swept hair, lean build, faceted low-poly painted style,
olive-green short tunic over cream sleeves, brown belt and trousers, brown
leather backpack, identical palette, scale, lighting, and camera distance.

## Revision 1

Generate an exact 2 by 2 grid of four side-facing walk frames toward image
right. In reading order: left-foot contact, passing pose, right-foot contact,
recovery pose. The first and third frames must show opposite colored boots
leading. Keep both boots visible, the full character inside each cell, and a
stable ground-contact line. No text, borders, separators, floor, shadow, or
effects.

## Revision 2

Repeat the same locked contract. In every frame require exactly one magenta
boot and exactly one cyan boot. Never draw two boots of the same color. Bind
each color to its anatomical leg throughout the cycle. Require at least one
boot width of separation in the two contact frames.

## Revision 3

Edit revision 2 while preserving the character and composition. Correct only
the boot binding and locomotion phases. Every cell must contain exactly one
magenta left boot and one cyan right boot. The first contact must have magenta
leading and the third contact must have cyan leading.
